Welcome to Our Organization!

Amrai Pari Open Scout Group is a Scouting organization of Bangladesh. It was established in 2019.

Virtual Education: Amrai Pari Foundation offers virtual education programs, which may include online courses, tutoring services, or distance learning opportunities. These programs can cover a wide range of subjects and are designed to enhance knowledge and skills among individuals of different age groups.

Library Facilities: The Foundation provides library facilities, allowing individuals to access a wide range of books, research materials, and other resources. The library may offer both physical and digital resources to cater to the diverse needs of its members.

Free Medical Services: Amrai Pari Foundation is dedicated to the well-being of its community members. As part of this commitment, it provides free medical services, which can include medical consultations, basic healthcare checkups, or preventive care programs. The goal is to ensure that individuals have access to essential healthcare services without financial barriers.

Overall, Amrai Pari Foundation focuses on providing a well-rounded experience to its members by offering virtual education, library resources, free medical services, and sports and exercise facilities. By encompassing these different aspects, the center aims to contribute to the educational, intellectual, physical, and overall well-being of the community it serves.
