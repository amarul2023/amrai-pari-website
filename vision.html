Amrai Pari Foundation(APF) is a non-profit organisation to focus on societies most pressing challenges. This foundation will primarily source funding from societies most fortunate groups, to initiate activities in order to enhance well-beings of most unfortunates. Secondary goals will be to monitor both private & public sectors activities and create awareness to improve all sectors for general people’s well-being. Other goals will be finding sensible living by caring environment without polluting water and air and stop all global warming acts. Finally Amrai Pari Foundation will try involving all groups from communities and create society with sensible income inequality and find initiatives lowering human sufferings.

OBJECTIVES

MAKF is a Bangladeshi non-government, non-profitable, non-political, voluntary and charitable-organization.
The objectives will be implemented after obtaining necessary permission from the Government/concerned or competent authority. brBefore implementation of the objectives, the objectives contrary to the provision of the Section 20 of the Foundation Registration Act 1860 shall be treated as ineffective. The objectives for which the Foundation is established are any or all of the followings.

  1. To undertake any humanitarian or charitable program for the poor, socio-economic and environmentally vulnerable and underprivileged people of the country.

  2. To explore and raise funds from internal and external sources for undertaking sustainable development programs with aims to health and environment promotion, livelihood security for the climate vulnerable communities and relief supports for the climate refuges, building community based disaster risk reduction capacity, community development and empowerment of women, increased access to education and life-skills resources and opportunities of the disadvantaged, and to poverty alleviation as a whole.

  3. To provide employment opportunities at higher wages through training of domestic workers and to make them competent for employment abroad so that they have to go abroad without any hassle and face various hardship later on.

  4. Establishment of pad Bank in schools with awareness of safe and hygienic menstruation for adolescent girls studying in different schools.

  5. Arranging for awareness and training for sexual abuse and self defense of adolescents.
  6. Raising awareness and training of women on safe and hygienic menstruation, sexual intercourse and various gynecological diseases. To provide services and financial assistance for the treatment of poor children, old and elderly women.

  7. Establish and operate “day care Centre” for the destitute children as well establish welfare Centre for children and youth.

  8. Invention of creative education system, establish and execute educational device and productive educational institution with the approval of concern authority. Providing scholarships among poor meritorious students and teaching scouting to develop the morality of the next generation.
  9. To train the people of coastal areas to reduce the damage caused by natural disasters and to work for their self improvement.

  10. To assist in the rehabilitation of adolescents and young people to aware of evils and abuse of drugs and rehabilitate them where necessary. To provide various training as well as technical training for self-employment of unemployed youth.

  11. Bringing people with disabilities into the mainstream of society through education training, rehabilitation and support.

  12. Adopt and distribution of spinning fund to take on different activities for the economical development of the community in our working area with the approval of the concern authority.
  13. Arrangements of seminar and workshop for the awareness and necessary trainings for the development of the community people livelihood.

  14. To arrange and establish Schools, Orphanage or similar other academic institutions to support human resource development aims of the Foundation.

  15. To form, organize and send voluntary team for the assistance, medical treatment of the victims of earthquake, floods, tidal surges, cyclones or any other natural calamities within the country and oversees.

  16. Observance of National and International days and to support democracy watch and election observation, if needed.

  17. To undertake motivation works and communication campaigns for creating mass awareness on education promotion, health and population, water and environment, disaster preparedness and management, science and technologies, etc.

  18. To organize and establish reading and study facilities for the interested community groups and students at local, regional, national and international levels.

  19. To receive all kinds of aid, grants, donations, technical or other form of assistances, subscriptions for the purpose of the Foundation. In case of foreign donations, grants, aids and assistance, compliance with the Foreign Donation (Voluntary Activities) Regulation Ordinance, 1978 shall be ensured.


It is hereby declared that the objectives as set forth in each and any of the above sub-clauses shall be regarded as separate and independent objectives of the Foundation, except where the context otherwise expressly requires, and shall in no way be limited or restricted by reference to or inference from the terms of any of the sub-clauses or the objectives therein specified or the powers thereby conferred shall be deemed merely subsidiary or auxiliary to the objectives mentioned herein above and the Foundation shall have full power to exercise or any of the powers conferred by any part of this clause.
